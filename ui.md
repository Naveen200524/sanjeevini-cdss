## **USER FLOWS**

### **Flow 1: Complete Analysis (Physician)**
```
1. Login â†’ Dashboard
2. Search patient (or scan ID)
3. Select patient from results
4. Click "New Analysis"
5. Choose test panel (or custom select)
6. Enter biomarker values
   - Auto-validation as typing
   - Red/yellow/green indicators
7. Review summary of entered values
8. Click "Analyze"
9. Wait 3-5 seconds (loading animation)
10. View results:
    - Overall health score
    - Risk predictions by disease
    - Trends vs previous
11. Click specific disease for details
12. View contributing factors (SHAP)
13. Review recommendations
14. Generate PDF report
15. Email to patient/add to EMR
16. Schedule follow-up (optional)
```

### **Flow 2: Lab Report Upload (Lab Technician)**
```
1. Login â†’ Lab Interface
2. Scan patient barcode
3. Click "Upload Lab Report"
4. Take photo or upload PDF
5. OCR extraction in progress (10-15 sec)
6. Review extracted values
   - Manually correct any errors
   - Highlight mismatches
7. Confirm and submit
8. Biomarkers auto-populated in system
9. Physician gets notification
10. Physician reviews and approves
```

### **Flow 3: Monitoring High-Risk Patient (Physician)**
```
1. Dashboard shows red alert badge (3)
2. Click notification icon
3. See list of high-risk patients
4. Click on "Rajesh Kumar - CVD 82%"
5. Quick view of risk increase timeline
6. Click "View Full Analysis"
7. See detailed risk factors
8. Add clinical note: "Discussed with patient, starting statin"
9. Set reminder: "Follow-up in 6 weeks"
10. Mark alert as "Acknowledged"
```
